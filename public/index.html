Powerful Tool To Manage Your Organization

Lok Kalyan Dwaar is a merit-based credit system that incentivizes productivity and encourages accountability and responsibility in the workplace.

Our Services

The primary objectives of the platform can be summarized as follows:

Optimizing workforce performance

The primary objective of Lok Kalyan Dwaar is to optimize the performance of the workforce by incentivizing employees to complete tasks efficiently and on time. By using a merit-based credit system, the platform promotes productivity, efficiency, and accountability, leading to better outcomes for organizations.

Explore page

Increasing transparency and fairness

The platform's analytics tools provide valuable insights into workforce productivity, allowing organizations to identify areas for improvement and address performance issues. By tracking employee performance with XP points, organizations can gain a comprehensive view of their workforce's productivity and identify high-performing employees.

Explore page

Promoting Meritocracy

The use of XP points in the Lok Kalyaan Dwaar platform promotes a merit-based culture where employees are evaluated based on their actual accomplishments, rather than just the number of years they have worked in the industry.
